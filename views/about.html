About me

I feel I have always carried a sense of entrepreneurial spirit throughout my life. This sense has been there… it’s been there all along believe me, but what could I do to activate it?

It’s always been one of those feelings you get when the word is on the tip of your tongue and you just can’t spit it out. In those moments it won’t come to mind, struggling with putting the pieces together, retracing steps and reversing your memory back and forth into a wall.

That’s what this sense was like to me up until recently. Just when I could finally spit it out and feel the weight lift off my shoulders.

I had to define myself.

To define yourself you have to know what you want to do and take the steps to push in that direction. It doesn’t matter when you do it, but it matters that you find it.

“Life is all about making the decision you feel is right in the moment and learning from the experience that the decision you made results in. There is no perfect way of doing things, there are only proven ways and methods of doing things, and sometimes even those are the wrong methods for you situation. Whatever you decide to do, stand by it with all of your heart and don’t let doubt or fear of failure push you down the wrong path.”


-Max Sendelin

Well... what exactly did I do?

I went to college, like everyone else. I had a great experience, but it wasn’t satisfactory because I had no idea what the hell I wanted to do with my life. I was pushing through a major that was just to get that piece of paper at the end of the road. I did end up graduating, but I didn’t find what defines me there.

Post college, countless jobs then-after, others telling me to get hop into careers they were passionate about, and failed attempt upon failed attempt at locating an actual career for myself. I figured out what work would truly define who I am.

I found freelance web development and a great community of creators doing what they love to do most.

I continually ask myself the same questions to this day.

“What exactly am I most passionate about though? What does this new found discovery push me toward? What’s next?”

Answering these questions I discovered I am most passionate about pushing the web toward a brighter future. Which brings me to Dev Anthem, a web development agency.

I believe this journey leads to the ultimate goal of creating something surrounded upon a larger idea than just a brand based upon myself. Dev Anthem is an agency not only for me, but also for my colleagues to be positive proponents of driving the future of progressive enhancement of the web.

Progressive Enhancement: What It Is, And How To Use It? – Smashing Magazine

I don’t want to be a CEO, I don’t want to be head of the board, I want to be apart of a cohort of creators building the web for the better. I continue forward with my passion for business, producing sites that are clean, minimal, easy to use, and maintainable.

I look forward to working together on your project!
